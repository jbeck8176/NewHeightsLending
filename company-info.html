Welcome To New Heights Lending

Bringing the expectations of lending to new heights!

New Heights Lending LLC is a Nevada owned and operated mortgage broker. With over 30 years of experience in the mortgage lending industry, New Heights Lending has the expertise needed to guide our borrowers with their residential financing needs. The company is currently licensed in California, Idaho, Montana, Nevada, New Mexico, Ohio, Pennsylvania, and Texas.

New Heights Lending LLC is an equal opportunity lender. Company NMLS for NV is 1160570, Company NMLS for OH is 1676109 and Company NMLS for TX is 1925458. New Heights Lending LLC is a member of the Las Vegas Metro Chamber of Commerce, the Better Business Bureau (the BBB), the National Federation of Independent Businesses (NFIB) and the Greater Las Vegas Association of Realtors (GLVAR).

Established in 2014, New Height's core team has worked together since 2005. The Company's focus is to embrace efficient workflow coupled with compliance minded, knowledgeable sales personnel to promote sustainable home ownership. As important as it is in helping our clients get into their homes or refinancing an existing loan, it is equally important to us that they have a full understanding of the process and feeling confident in their choices.

New Heights Lending is bringing the expectations of lending to new heights… and you can rest assured that our motto is not just a tagline, it’s a promise!
